## 🚀 About Me

I'm Tarun Binay Das, a CSE undergrad who loves to build, design, and experiment. Whether it's developing apps, designing intuitive UI/UX, creating digital art, or diving into game dev — I find joy in turning ideas into interactive experiences.

I'm particularly passionate about blending technology with creativity, crafting solutions that not only work well but also look and feel engaging.

**Currently:**

- 🎨 Graphics Lead @ SKEPSIS
- 💼 Intern @ Indiminds Technologies LLP
- 🎮 Exploring Game Development & 3D Modeling
- 🛠️ Building apps that solve real problems
- 🌱 Volunteering & sharing what I learn
